Yellow (The Bee)

The boat is a 2-seater, High-Performance Tornado RIB 5.4 from 2018. Complete with a 100HP/75kW Mercury 4-stroke engine (the maximum allowed).
Equipped with an attachable ladder, anchor and options for Water Skiing, Wakeboards etc. this can be a fun boat as well as practical.

The electronic map computer displays course, navigation, water depths and more and with a 55 liters fuel tank there is enough fuel for many hours on the water.
